Startup Panel

Varun Arora, Karan Sikka, Ilter Canberk, Chris Leaf and Sarah Tan

Description

Come hear what representatives from local startups have to say about their experiences, their companies, what worked, and what didn't in this question-and-answer style session.

Our wonderful speakers will be:

  • Varun Arora from Open Curriculum
  • Karan Sikka and Ilter Canberk from Appcubator
  • Chris Leaf from Thinkerous
  • Sarah Tan from Exadler Technologies

Be sure to come with lots of questions!
