FAQ

What is CrashCourse?

CrashCourse is a series of tech-related workshops hosted by ScottyLabs. Fellow talented CMU students will be leading these action-packed workshops, so you will have the opportunity to work closely with people who have actually applied knowledge learned within classroom in a real-world settings such as hackathons, internships, and careers.

We cater to all levels of experience with topics ranging from Intro to HTML/CSS to Advanced Computer Security, making this the perfect opportunity for anyone wanting to expand their skill set!

When is CrashCourse?

This year, we're splitting CrashCourse over two Saturdays, October 25th and November 1st. There will be different talks for each weekend, so be sure to check out the schedule below! Both sessions start at 11:30 AM and end approximately at 8 PM. While we invite you to stay for the entire day, but we completely understand if you have to leave at an earlier time.

Who can participate in CrashCourse?

We welcome any CMU student willing to learn to CrashCourse! We especially encourage non-SCS students to come on by! Our beloved sponsors (TBA) will also be at the November 1st event! They will be hosting a series of tech talks over dinner and entertain any of your questions. Stay tuned!

What do I need to bring?

Need: Laptop charger, and an open mind! We'll be taking care of all your swag and snacking needs. Also, check out the workshop pages as some might require specific software!
